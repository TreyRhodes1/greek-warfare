Timeline

July 356 BC: Birth of Alexander in Macedonia to King Philip and Queen Olympias

340 BC: First victory and establishment of the colony, Alexandroupolis

336 BC: King Philip assassinated

335 BC: Settled revolt in Thebes, conquering the city-state in the process

334 BC: First major victory against the Persians

333 BC: Won the battle of Issus

332 BC: Took Gaza by force
